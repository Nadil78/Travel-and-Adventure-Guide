vesak festival

Celebrated by both the Sinhalese and Tamil communities, this festival marks the traditional New Year. It signifies the end of the harvest season and the arrival of the sun in the astrological sign of Aries.Traditional customs, rituals, games, and family gatherings. The festival includes preparing sweet dishes like kiribath (milk rice), playing traditional games such as kotta pora (pillow fighting), and lighting the hearth at an auspicious time. Celebrates the birth, enlightenment, and death of the Buddha. Vesak is one of the most important Buddhist festivals in Sri Lanka.Streets and homes are adorned with colorful lanterns, pandals (illuminated displays), and oil lamps. Temples hold special sermons and meditation sessions, and dansals (free food stalls) offer refreshments to passersby.
